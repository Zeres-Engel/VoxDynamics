# Centroid Calibration Report: Phase 1

This report documents the performance improvement after applying **Dynamic Centroid Calibration**. Instead of using theoretical centroids, we moved the emotional "centers" to the actual means calculated from 1,441 samples in the target dataset.

## 2. Technical Insight: Why 34%?
Even with calibrated centroids, the accuracy remains under 40% because:
1.  **Cluster Overlap**: In the Wav2Vec2 V-A-D space, `Angry` and `Surprised` are extremely close. Without spectral cleaning, the AI cannot tell "Loud and Happy" apart from "Loud and Angry".
2.  **Audio Noise**: The input files have varying silences and background noise levels which jitter the VAD coordinates.
3.  **Low Valence Sensitivity**: The underlying Wav2Vec2 model seems to prioritize Arousal over Valence, making it hard to distinguish positive vs. negative emotions of similar energy.

## 3. Next Research Steps

> [!TIP]
> **Experiment 2: Audio Normalization & Noise Gating**
> By cleaning the audio signal *before* it reaches the Wav2Vec2 model, we expect to stabilize the VAD output and push accuracy towards 50-60%.
